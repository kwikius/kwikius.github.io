Design

Weight

The boat should be light for speed .. however if the boat is too light she reacts so fast to gusts etc that she is difficult to control, without lightning reactions.
On the other hand a heavy boat needs a lot of buoyancy in the floats. Most succesful Mini40s seem to weigh about 5lbs.

Stiffness

With a conventional main/headsail arrangement it is important to keep tension in the forestay.
This is much more difficult in a multihull than a monohull, and is the main reason why carbon is often used for the crossbeams etc.
