The great side menu debate

Some pages on the site contain side menus.

There has been an ongoing argument among the designers as to which side to put the side menus.
In order to settle this argument we have provided this page.

Click the boxes below to display the side menus on the left or right of the main page.

note1: The information is stored in a "cookie" on your PC and will be valid for the next 10 days. After that the cookie will be deleted automatically and the default(right) will appear again. If it doesnt seem to work ... check your browser has cookies enabled.

note2: This is an Experimental page. It has been tested in IE5, NN4, NN6, Opera4, Opera5. After changing the side of the menu, using the [back] or [refresh] button may cause recently visited pages on the site with side menus to display incorrectly.
Reloading the errant page via the top menu button for the page should bring the page to light correctly. However the results cannot be predicted for all browsers.
